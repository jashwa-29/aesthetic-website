Skin Booster Injection

  • Home
  • Medspa
  • Skin Rejuvenation
  • Skin Booster Injection

Skin booster injections are a very popular Korean treatment that focuses on achieving flawless, glowing skin. A wide range of complexion concerns can be addressed with these injections. 

Silky-Smooth Skin

Skin booster injections are treatments that are administered superficially into the skin. They are ideal for men and women looking to enhance their skin quality. They often contain ingredients that our bodies already have; we just use them to provide a “boost” to improve the skin’s appearance. Skin boosters retain moisture consistently, stimulate new collagen, and provide long-lasting skin that is smooth and radiant. Skin boosters contain different ingredients, with hyaluronic acid being the most common.

What are Skin Booster Injections Like?

Patients can receive skin booster injections in the face, neck, décolletage, or on the backs of their hands. We will first cleanse your skin, and then a topical numbing cream can be applied if needed. The injections are quite shallow, with very fine needles being used. Treatment typically involves three sessions, with a fourth touch-up session within the year. You can expect needle marks and a little bruising for 3-4 days following treatment. 

What Can Skin Booster Injections Do?

  • Hydrates and repairs the skin
  • Improves skin elasticity, tone, and texture
  • Stimulates collagen production 
  • Restores the skin’s natural luminosity
  • Tightens the skin
  • Reduces wrinkles
  • Shrinks pores
  • Provides a watery glow to the skin
  • Whitens and brightens the skin
  • Lightens scars over time
  • Contours the face

Customizable Treatments

Skin booster injections are very customizable to your specific beauty concerns and skin type. They even work for sensitive skin. If you’re looking for a glass-like radiance for your skin, boosters are a great option. They are also often used to administer growth factors to make the skin softer, and they can also be used to prevent wrinkles, whiten the skin for an incredibly fair appearance, and provide anti-aging benefits. Sometimes a multitude of ingredients are used (similar to IV treatments) including antioxidants, minerals, amino acids, hyaluronic acid, vitamins, and more. Sometimes skin booster injections can be used to break down and destroy fat cells to contour the chin, cheeks, and cheekbones so that a round jawline is slimmed into a sharper “V” line. Some patients even choose to have boosters to lift tissue and muscles along the mouth to achieve a “permanent” subtle smile. No matter what you’re looking for, we have the skin booster injection to help.

Results

After your skin booster injections, you will notice an immediate improvement in the tone, texture, and feel of your skin.

Elasticity and luminosity will be enhanced, and will continue to look better and better with each treatment. The longevity of your results will depend on your age, lifestyle, and skin type. We can give you a better idea of how long your results will last during your consultation.
