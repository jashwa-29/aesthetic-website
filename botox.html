According to the American Society of Plastic Surgeons (ASPS), BOTOX (botulinum toxin type A) is the number one minimally-invasive cosmetic procedure in the nation. In a recent year, 7 million BOTOX treatments were performed in the U.S.A.

BOTOX is a popular cosmetic treatment for a reason. It is a safe, effective, minimally-invasive procedure to soften wrinkles caused by muscle movement. At Cara Plastic Surgery & Laser Center, we perform BOTOX injections in Los Angeles to treat lines and wrinkles on the forehead, around the eyes, between the brows, and vertical lines in the neck.

Am I A Candidate For Botox?

If you are struggling with age-related dynamic wrinkles and you seek a safe, non-surgical solution with little to no downtime, BOTOX could be the right option for you. Good candidates for treatment are over 18 years old, are physically healthy, and are realistic in what they expect BOTOX can do for them. Clients who are pregnant or nursing or who suffer from neuromuscular diseases are better suited to different approaches.

What Is Botox?

BOTOX is a brand name for botulinum toxin type A. There are other similar products like BOTOX such as Dysport and Xeomin. When small, diluted amounts are injected directly into the muscles, it causes controlled weakening of the muscle. BOTOX blocks the signal from the nerves to the muscles.

When the muscle is weakened, it no longer contracts, and wrinkles begin to relax and soften. BOTOX has been approved by the FDA for treating moderate to severe crow’s feet and frown lines between the eyebrows. It is also frequently used to treat other facial areas.

What Can I Expect From My Results?

Results with BOTOX last 2-5 months and sometimes longer. Your skin will appear smooth, taut, and youthful for that period, and people may comment on how well-rested and relaxed you appear. You can repeat your treatment as needed to maintain your positive new image.

What Can I Expect From My Recovery?

BOTOX is a non-surgical, non-invasive procedure that takes just minutes to perform. Clients can return immediately to their regular schedule after their injections. Side effects are rare but may include some minor swelling, bruising, and tenderness at the treatment site. These symptoms usually evaporate within 24 hours at most.

BOTOX Procedure

A BOTOX in Los Angeles & Orange County can be performed in a few minutes with no anesthesia required. Our plastic surgeon uses very fine needles to inject BOTOX into specific muscles. Patients experience only minor discomfort with this treatment.

You may return to work and normal activities immediately after the procedure. It generally takes 3 to 7 days for maximum results of the treatment to appear. Depending on the dosage, BOTOX treatments can last up to 3 or 4 months.

Muscles that are not in use tend to weaken over time. This may also apply to muscles that have been injected with BOTOX, which may result in less BOTOX needed to achieve cosmetic goals in the future.

Jaw Contouring With Botox

When jaw muscles used for chewing are oversized, it can cause the face to appear square-shaped. For patients with overdeveloped jaw muscles who want a slender V shape, BOTOX injections can provide non-surgical jaw contouring. These treatments can reduce the size of the jaw muscles to create a more gently curved jawline and a more oval-shaped facial contour. BOTOX can also alleviate TMJ and nighttime teeth grinding pains.

Reasons to Have BOTOX
