ellacor is an innovative approach to addressing multiple cosmetic concerns through the utilization of micro-coring technology. The system uses specialized hollow needles to gently extract skin cells from the treatment area, stimulating the skin's natural healing processes. ellacor in Los Angeles & Orange County extends its benefits to individuals dealing with mild to severe wrinkles in the mid to lower face area.

How does ellacor work?

The ellacor® Systeme uses hollow needles to remove micro-cores of skin, resulting in an improved appearance of moderate and severe wrinkles in the mid to lower face without evidence of scarring.

Are you a candidate for ellacor?

The ellacor System featuring Micro-Coring® technology is designed for utilization by skilled medical practitioners who will assess your skin type suitability to achieve optimal outcomes.

It is intended to address moderate and severe wrinkles in the mid and lower facial areas of adults aged 22 years or above, with skin ranging from fair to moderately tan.

What are the benefits of ellacor? 

Advanced Skin Rejuvenation: ellacor micocoring technology utilizes microscopic coring to trigger the skin's natural healing processes.

Swift Treatment Duration: ellacor treatments take as little as 15 to 30 minutes. 

Gradual Improvement: Patients experience noticeable results within days after treatment, with further reductions in fine lines and skin sagging by the 2-to-3-month mark.

What is the recovery process after an ellacor treatment?

Recovery time ranges from a few days to weeks, which can vary by patient and treatment settings. Post-treatment, you might notice mild redness or slight swelling in treated areas.

Many individuals seamlessly transition back into their routines, thanks to the convenience offered by ellacor. Results often start emerging within a few weeks, continuously enhancing over the ensuing months as your body's natural processes take the reins.

How many ellacor treatments will I need?

The frequency of ellacor treatments varies depending on individual skin concerns and goals. Generally, patients observe significant results after 2 to 6 sessions spaced approximately 30 days apart.
