Meet Dr. Bharathi Rajasridhar– Leading Plastic & Cosmetic Surgeon in Chennai

Dr. Bharathi R., the consultant plastic and cosmetic surgeon, is a qualified plastic surgeon with over 19 years of experience. Her qualifications include an MBBS, MS in General Surgery, and MCh in Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery from the prestigious Madras Medical College.

She specializes in various cosmetic procedures and has gained expertise and experience in Breast surgeries, including Implants, Enhancement and Augmentation surgeries, Liposuction, and Gynecomastia correction.

She is highly rated for her communication and commitment to her patients in all the reviews.
