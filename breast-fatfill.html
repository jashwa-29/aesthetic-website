What Is Breast Fat Fill?

Breast fat fill, also called autologous fat grafting, is a natural breast enhancement technique that uses your own body fat to increase breast volume and improve shape. This innovative procedure provides a subtle, natural-looking enhancement while avoiding artificial implants.

How Does It Work?

  • Fat is gently removed from donor areas (abdomen, thighs, etc.) using liposuction
  • The fat is carefully processed and purified
  • Healthy fat cells are injected into the breasts in precise layers
  • The body naturally retains a portion of the transferred fat

Advantages of Fat Transfer

  • Natural results - Uses your own tissue for a soft, natural feel
  • Dual benefit - Enhances breasts while contouring donor areas
  • No implants - Avoids foreign materials and associated risks
  • Minimal scarring - Only tiny injection points required
  • Faster recovery - Compared to traditional augmentation

Ideal Candidate

You may be a good candidate for breast fat fill if you:

  • Want a modest increase in breast volume (typically 1-1.5 cup sizes)
  • Prefer a natural alternative to breast implants
  • Have sufficient donor fat available
  • Want to correct mild asymmetry
  • Seek natural-looking, proportionate results
  • Want to avoid visible scars

The Breast Fat Fill Procedure

Our natural breast enhancement technique is performed in three key steps for optimal results:

Step 1: Fat Harvesting

Using gentle liposuction techniques, fat is carefully removed from predetermined areas like the abdomen, thighs, or flanks. The donor sites are chosen based on your body shape and fat availability.

Step 2: Fat Processing

The collected fat is specially processed to isolate the healthiest fat cells, removing impurities, fluids, and damaged cells. This ensures only viable fat cells are used for transfer.

Step 3: Fat Injection

Using micro-cannulas, the purified fat is strategically injected in small amounts throughout the breast tissue in multiple layers for smooth, even, natural-looking results.

Procedure Details: 2-3 hours duration | Daycare procedure (no overnight stay) | Light activities in 3-5 days | Full recovery in 2-3 weeks
