Body

  • Body

Nature gives us a specific body contour – but modern surgical techniques make it possible to resculpt your body to create a more appealing shape. At Cara Plastic Surgery & Laser Center, we focus on producing the most appealing body shape possible for each individual we treat.

Whether you are unhappy with the appearance of sagging muscles, tissue, and fat in the tummy area, envision a body lift to restore a more youthful look, or want to enhance your buttocks or the appearance of your genital area, we offer a full spectrum of treatments to create a more beautiful body.

Liposuction

Liposuction

While liposuction has been performed for years, newer technology produces superior results. Dr. Kahng is committed to providing his patients with the most advanced technology, including the systems he uses to remove excess fat from various body areas and enhance with skin tightening by using a laser under the skin.

Tummy Tuck

Tummy Tuck

The passage of time, pregnancy, or weight loss can leave your tummy area looking saggy, loose, and unattractive. If you are tired of seeing excess skin, fat, and tissue in your abdomen, a tummy tuck could be the answer. Restore and rejuvenate your entire body contour, and create a firmer, more youthful body profile with the help of leading Korean plastic surgeon, Dr. David Kahng.

Brazilian Butt Lift

Brazilian Butt Lift

A BBL offers patients the opportunity to remove stubborn fat while also enhancing the size and shape of their backside. During the procedure, fat is removed from the area of your choice and used to reshape and define the butt.

Non-Surgical Butt Lift

Non-Surgical Butt Lift

A rounder or more full backside can create a flattering natural contour. Unfortunately, not everyone is born with natural curves. A non-surgical butt lift can rejuvenate your lower curves without the time and cost of a more invasive surgical procedure. 

Mommy Makeover

Mommy Makeover

Motherhood brings countless joys, but can leave a woman’s body looking older, with sagging skin and muscles in the tummy area, breasts that have lost volume and look deflated, and stretch marks. For a customized restoration of your breasts with an augmentation/lift and abdomen with a tummy tuck/liposuction, a mommy makeover can help you bring back the youthful, firm look you had prior to having children.

Body Lift

Body Lift

Are you tired of seeing the signs of age on your body, with droopy, saggy skin and tissue? A body lift is a customized surgical treatment to address your individual aesthetic problems and restore a firmer, more youthful body contour.

Labiaplasty

Labiaplasty

Our most private areas can be an embarrassment when the structures and tissues are unusual. This can happen with or without pregnancy, young or old. A labiaplasty can help you have a natural, pleasing look in your genital area by removing excess skin and tissue. If your inner lips protrude or your outer lips look odd or saggy, a labiaplasty and fat grafting can help you feel a greater sense of confidence in the appearance of this intimate area.

Gluteal Implants

Gluteal Implants

Gluteal implants can be used to increase the size of your backside. The implants used during this surgical procedure mimic the natural tissue in the area to create a more defined shape and add a pleasing contour to your frame.
